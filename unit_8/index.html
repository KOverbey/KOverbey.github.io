Overbey Book Designs

Building better books.

Overbey Book Designs was built with one purpose in mind: to bring
affordable, thoughtful book design and editorial services to authors in need.

ebook publishing

  • professional layout starting from Word, PDF, or other digital document
  • high-quality files compatible with every ereader (Kindle, iPad, NOOK, etc.)

  • customized with the theme and story of your book in mind
  • creation of all necessary files including .epub, .mobi, and .pdf

print book design

  • customized layout that meets industry standards

  • proofs always sent for review and approval

  • services available for ficiton and non-fiction

  • thorough quality check always included

editorial

  • line edit: intensive structural edit focusing on tone, style, language, etc.
  • copy edit: word-by-word edit considering sentence fluency and consistency
  • proofread: word-by-word edit with attention paid specifically to punctuation and grammar
