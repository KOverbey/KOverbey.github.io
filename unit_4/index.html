Overbey Book Designs was built with one purpose in mind: to bring
affordable, thoughtful book design and editorial services to authors in need.

Services

ebook publishing

  • professional layout starting from Word, PDF, or other digital document
  • high-quality files compatible with every ereader (Kindle, iPad, NOOK, etc.)
  • customized with the theme and story of your book in mind
  • creation of all necessary files including .epub, .mobi, and .pdf
  • thorough quality check always included
  • proofs always sent for review and approval

interior book design

  • will work with your input to meet the theme and feel of your book
  • end result is customized layout that meets industry standards
  • proofs always sent for review and approval
  • readers will have an easy, enjoyable experience
  • services available for highly designed, full-color books (art, photography, cookbooks, children's books)
  • services available for general black-and-white fiction/non-fiction titles
  • thorough quality check always included

book editing services

  • line edit: intensive structural edit focusing on tone, style, language, etc.
  • copy editing: word-by-word edit considering grammar, usage, and consistency
  • proofreading: word-by-word edit with attention paid specifically to punctuation and grammar
  • close consultation of Merriam-Webster Dictionary and style manuals
  • proficient with word processors and PDF proofing
  • thorough quality check always included and style sheet always provided
  • hourly rates vary depending on service
